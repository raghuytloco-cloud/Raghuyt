Linking your in-game accounts to Stan ties your real gaming identity to your profile. Once linked, your BGMI and Free Fire stats become visible to the community, your profile earns a verified-player badge, and you unlock game-specific community features such as squad boards and leaderboards.

## Why Link Your Game Accounts

Connecting a game account to your Stan profile gives you:

- **Stats display** — your in-game K/D ratio, rank, and season stats appear directly on your Stan profile card.
- **Verified identity** — other players can confirm you are who you say you are by cross-referencing your in-game ID.
- **Community features** — squad finder, game-specific rooms, and ranked leaderboards all require a verified game account.

## Find Your Player ID

Before you link an account, you need your numeric Player ID from the game itself.

### BGMI Player ID

1. Open **Battlegrounds Mobile India** on your device.
2. Tap your **avatar / profile picture** in the top-left corner of the lobby.
3. Your **Player ID** (a numeric string) appears beneath your in-game name on the profile screen.

### Free Fire Player ID

1. Open **Free Fire** on your device.
2. Tap your **profile icon** in the top-left corner of the home screen.
3. Your **UID** (numeric Player ID) is displayed just below your nickname.

<Note>
  Write your Player ID down before switching apps — you will need to enter it exactly in the Stan app during the linking step.
</Note>

## Link a Game Account in the Stan App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Launch the **Stan** app and tap your profile avatar in the bottom navigation bar to open your profile. Then tap the **Settings** (gear) icon in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Game Accounts">
    Scroll down to the **Linked Accounts** section and tap **Game Accounts**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the game to link">
    Tap **Link BGMI** or **Link Free Fire**, depending on which account you want to connect.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your in-game Player ID">
    Type your numeric Player ID into the input field exactly as it appears in the game, then tap **Continue**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify ownership">
    Stan will prompt you to complete a short in-game verification step (such as setting a temporary in-game name token) to confirm you own the account. Follow the on-screen instructions, then tap **Verify**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the link">
    Once verification succeeds, tap **Save**. Your game account is now linked to your Stan profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Only **one BGMI account** and **one Free Fire account** can be linked to a single Stan profile at a time. Linking a new account will replace any previously linked account for that game.
</Warning>

## What Changes After Linking

After you successfully link a game account, two things happen automatically:

1. **Your Stan profile** — the `bgmiProfileId` and/or `freefireProfileId` fields on your profile object become populated with your numeric in-game ID.
2. **Your session JWT** — the token issued on your next login will include the linked profile IDs in its payload, so downstream integrations can read them without an extra API call.

A linked profile response looks like this:

```json
{
  "id": 32091378,
  "username": "ItzDakshbro1",
  "displayName": "Daksh",
  "bgmiProfileId": 59704785,
  "freefireProfileId": 59704786,
  "countryCode": "in",
  "avatarUrl": "https://cdn.getstan.app/avatars/32091378.jpg"
}
```

When a game account is **not** linked, the corresponding field (`bgmiProfileId` or `freefireProfileId`) will be `null`.

<Tip>
  Verify that linking worked by fetching your own profile from the API and checking that the game profile ID fields are non-null:

  ```bash
  curl --http2 -X GET \
    -H "Host: api.getstan.app" \
    -H "accept: application/json, text/plain, */*" \
    -H "gaid: <YOUR_GAID>" \
    -H "appversion: 253" \
    -H "stallionversion: 0" \
    -H "platform: android" \
    -H "sid: <YOUR_SID>" \
    -H "ts: <UNIX_MS_TIMESTAMP>" \
    -H "appinstanceid: <YOUR_APPINSTANCEID>" \
    -H "installreferrer: utm_source=google-play&utm_medium=organic" \
    -H "countrycode: in" \
    -H "fingerprintid: <YOUR_FINGERPRINTID>" \
    -H "authorization: Bearer <YOUR_TOKEN>" \
    -H "accept-encoding: gzip" \
    -H "user-agent: okhttp/4.12.0" \
    "https://api.getstan.app/api/v1/users?username=<YOUR_USERNAME>"
  ```

  If `bgmiProfileId` or `freefireProfileId` is populated in the response, the link is active.
</Tip>

## Unlinking a Game Account

To remove a linked game account, return to **Settings → Game Accounts**, tap the linked account, and choose **Unlink**. The corresponding profile ID field will return to `null` on your next profile fetch.
